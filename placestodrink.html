Tip Top Ten Bath
  • About Bath
  • Places to Visit
  • Places to Eat
  • Places to Drink
1. Sub 13

Stylish cellar bar, with intimate spaces and a courtyard, serving cocktails, champagnes and spirits.

2. Opium Bar

Boutique watering hole with pan-historical decor offering an extensive beer and cocktail menu.

3. Be At One

Lively chain bar that serves sweet, sour and spicy cocktails, and offers mixing masterclasses.

4. Belushi's Bath

Informal chain bar with TV sport and live entertainment, plus a burger menu and global comfort food.

5. Hall & Woodhouse

Chic bar and British restaurant with a modern vintage vibe in 4 airy rooms with a roof terrace.

6. The Bath Brew House

Rustic-chic pub with on-site brewery offering spitroast dishes from an open kitchen, plus garden.

7. The Cork

Cosy upstairs bar with hearty fare and tapas, plus sleek downstairs cocktail bar and heated terrace.

8. Cosy Club

Industrial-chic meets kitsch Victoriana in large quirky cafe/bar serving grill and tapas dishes.

9. Slug & Lettuce

Smart chain pub with a cocktail list, a classic British menu and uncluttered contemporary decor.

10. Lamb & Lion

Hearty, all-day comfort food, plus live TV sports and large garden in no-frills, regency-built pub.
