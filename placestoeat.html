1. Ponte Vecchio

Bath's most famous view is right in front of this bustling comtemporary Italian restaurant.

2. Scallop Shell

Colourful distressed furniture fills this wood-dominated venue specialising in fish and seafood.

3. Wild Cafe

Corner eatery with wood-tipped tables plus food made from local products and organic ingredients.

4. Sotto Sotto

Classic Italian menu with a contemporary twist in candlelit vaulted cellers with bare stone walls.

5. Ole Tapas

If you are looking for fresh Spanish food and drinks in a fun and relaxed environment, Ole Tapas offers the winning formula.

6. Bath Noodles

The best noodles and asian food in Bath!!

7. The Pump Rooms

Crumbly scones, luscious clotted cream and sweet, fruity jams: this is the go-to locations for Afternoon Tea.

8. Sally Lunn's

Bath's oldest house (c.1483), home of the famous Sally Lunn Bun (a semi-sweet bread) and tearoom.

9. Thai Balcony

Serves luxury authentic Thai cuisine.

10. Clayton's Kitchen

Serving traditional British dishes with a modern twist in a contemporarily designed Georgian Townhouse.
